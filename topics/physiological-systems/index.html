Perception / Physiological Systems

Physiological sensing viewed as measurement of adaptive biological systems.

Physiological Systems is the topic program focused on human-centered sensing, regulation, and biosignal interpretation. It treats measurements like ECG, EEG, EDA, respiration, and PPG as partial observations of multiscale, adaptive, and coupled biological dynamics.

Scope
Dynamics

Multiscale regulation, observability, state, and physiological coupling.

Measurement

Biosignal acquisition, interpretation, and device-aware physiological observation.

Analysis

Classical and advanced signal-processing approaches for physiological data.
