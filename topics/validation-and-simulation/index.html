Action / Validation and Simulation

Evidence, benchmarking, and simulation for trustworthy embodied systems.

Validation and Simulation is the topic program focused on how embodied systems are benchmarked, stress-tested, calibrated, and argued from evidence. It sits inside the Action pillar as the program for benchmarking, safety architecture, digital twins, and system-level risk measurement.

Scope
Benchmarking

Scenario design, structured variability, intervention-aware metrics, and evidence-producing protocols.

Safety Evidence

Evaluation-by-design, failure modes, propagation risk, and runtime admissibility.

Simulation

Digital twins, runtime calibration, system identification, and sim-to-real accountability.
